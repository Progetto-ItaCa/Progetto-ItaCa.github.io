# {{ page.title }} 

Since 2019, ItaCa organizes an annual workshop meant to gather the Italian community of category theorists. 
The focus lies on the development and applications of category theory.

* [ItaCa 2025, Milan](https://progetto-itaca.github.io/ItaCa-25/)
* [ItaCa 2024, Padoa](https://progetto-itaca.github.io/ItaCa-24/)
* [ItaCa 2023, Turin](https://progetto-itaca.github.io/ItaCa-23/)
* [ItaCa 2022, Pisa](https://progetto-itaca.github.io/ItaCa-22/)
* [ItaCa 2021, Genoa](https://genoa-logic-group.github.io/itaca-workshop-2021/)
* [ItaCa 2019, Milan](http://www.mat.unimi.it/users/itaca/)
